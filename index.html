Biography

 Popularly known as Unique Kid, Joseph Grace, a native of Ogbedigbo in Delta State was born in Kwale town and had both primary and secondary education in her place of birth. Finished her Higher National Diploma from the Polytechnic of Ibadan. She is from a family of eight, five boys and three girls. A very free-spirited lady, full of love and exitement for everyone in her world. Never intimidated by people's successes. She is highly diplomatic in dealing with people and doesn't allow everyone in her space as she is always careful the kind of people she lets in. So God fearing and and practices what she believes in.
